What i like to do 

These are just a couple of my interest 

Camping

Camping is one of my favorite things because it takes you out to explore God's creation. Things that I like to do while camping are going on hikes and fishing. 

Shopping

I like going out to shop and finding things that I think are cool. I like to either shop online or thrift because there are clothes and other pieces that many other people do not have 

Traveling

I love to travel the world. I've been to 19 countries so far and I hope to go to many more. I love to learn about the different cultures and exploring the countries that I visit.

About my life

  • Winston-Salem, NC

    Born in 2005

    I lived here for the beginning of my life. I have no memories that I can recall about this place. 

  • Greenville, South Carolina

    From 2006-now

    In Greenville is where I lived the majority of my life. I went to elementary school at Pelham Road Elementary school. For middle school and high school I went to Bob Jones Academy and now I am going to college at Bob Jones University. 

  • Schroon Lake, New York

    2023-2024

    I went to Word of Life Bible Institute in the Upstate New York. Here is where I studied the Bible and worked in and with many different ministries.  

  • Anywhere in the world

    Future

    One of my favorite activities is I love to travel so one thing that I would love to do is either have a job where they allow me to travel or a remote job where I could work from anywhere in the world.
